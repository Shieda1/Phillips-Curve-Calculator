What is the phillips curve calculator?
The phillips curve calculator is a tool that finds the relationship between inflation and other Phillips curve model variables.
How to calculate phillips curve?
To calculate phillips curve you should use the following formula:
change in unemployment rate = trend rate of growth of money wages - percentage rate of money wage growth
